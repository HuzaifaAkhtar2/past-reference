About Myself

My name is Huzaifa Akhtar and I am from Pakistan. I've created this website for students of SSC-I and SSC-II,
because many students find difficulties in finding past papers for good exam preparation.
My motto is to provide students swift access to these papers because they are often very useful in exam preparation.
This website provides you model papers, past papers and E-Sheets for free and no need to create account.
But this website only provides past papers of Federal Board Pakistan and it provides only papers of SSC-I and SSC-II.
I will try to improve this site by adding more papers for students. And I can understand diificulties of students because I am itself a student.
